Process & Timeline

Most websites are completed in 2-3 weeks from kickoff to launch. This timeline assumes you can provide content and feedback promptly. Complex projects with custom features or AI agent integrations may take 4-6 weeks. We'll give you a specific timeline during our initial consultation based on your requirements.

Every project includes 2 rounds of revisions. In our experience, this is plenty when we nail the strategy upfront. Each revision round lets you request changes to design, layout, or content. If you need additional revisions beyond that, we can accommodate at an hourly rate — but honestly, it's rare.

To kick off your project, we need three things: your content (text, images, videos), brand assets (logo, colors, fonts if you have them), and your goals (what success looks like for this website). Don't worry if you don't have everything — we can help with copywriting and source stock imagery if needed.
